—— 02 Studio

A small studio with a long memory.

Founded in 2008, Concept Architects has remained deliberately compact — never more than twelve people, never more than seven live projects.

Every commission is led personally by Peter from first sketch to handover key. We don't subcontract design, we don't repeat plans, and we don't take on more than we can resolve at full fidelity.

Our clients come to us because they have already seen the other studios. They stay because the conversation is honest, the drawings are accurate, and the buildings — when they're finally inhabited — feel inevitable.

—— 03 Approach

Five conversations, then a house.

  1. 01

    Brief

    A long, slow afternoon at your existing home, or where you'd like the new one to stand. We listen more than we draw.

  2. 02

    Concept

    Two or three radically different propositions, built as physical models and atmosphere boards. One is chosen, not designed by committee.

  3. 03

    Resolution

    Full design development in-house: structure, climate, every joint detailed by the same hand that drew the concept.

  4. 04

    Site

    We're on site weekly. Site decisions live with the architect, not the contractor's commercial lead.

  5. 05

    Aftercare

    A five-year relationship: we return for the snag walk, the first winter, the second summer, the day you change the planting scheme.
